WORK & STUDY
As a Computer Science graduate and a two Masters degree holder I have been thoroughly studying and experimenting with web technologies, in 2005 I have earned excellence for developing a website for my department at Al Nahrain University and in 2011 I have developed OASIS a customized web application that involved advanced techniques like Object Oriented Programming and Database Modeling for students application to Iraqi universities.

Then I started work as a web developer in a well known TV channel in Dubai Media City. I tried hard to use my academic knowledge to stand out from the competition by designing and implementing a unique website with advanced Graphical User Interfaces.

I am currently studying for PHD and working as a GA at the University of Arkansas at Little Rock. I am proudly happy to be working as part of the group COSMOS, my duties include the design and development of the UI for two softwares that track blogs and videos for malicious behaviors(YOUTUBETRACKER & BLOGTRACKERS). COSMOS is funded from the U.S. National Science Foundation (NSF), U.S. Office of Naval Research (ONR), U.S. Air Force Research Lab (AFRL), U.S. Army Research Office (ARO), and U.S. Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency (DARPA).
