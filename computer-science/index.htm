College of Education

The sixth conference of the education college

Educational and Psychological Sciences "Renewals and Future Applications"

22-24 /11/2005
Arbid - Jordan

A preface

The increasing progress in the age the globalization and the cognitive explosion in the field of the information technology and the criteria culture imposes terrific challenges in the field of the educational and psychological sciences in all of it's different sides. Therefore it was a duty on the supervisors of this field in Jordan and the Arabian world to repeat their view about it, and to intensify their efforts and direct them for the renewals of future applications in the educational and psychological field of science, with what agrees with the age spirit and its developments, and handles its latest developments and challenges.

Targets of the conference :
1- Following-up the international directions in the educational and psychological field of science with the analytic study.
2- Defining the development priorities and the renewal in the field of the educational and psychological sciences.
3- Arab and international experience exchange regarding the educational and psychological renewals and their applications.
4- Showing the renewals and the future applications in the field of the educational and psychological sciences, and the clarification of their effect in the operation of the complete development.

Axes of the conference :
First : The curricula and the teaching ways, renewals and applications
1- The computerization of the curricula.
2- The education models.
3- Technology of teaching.
4- The preparation of the teachers and their qualification.
5- The curricula and the knowledge economy.
Second : Psychological science : renewals and applications
1- Psychological science and the integrated social upbringing of the children.
2- Psychological science and the revolution of knowledge, technology and media.
3- Psychological science and the human development.
4- Psychological science and the interior and exterior political situations.
Third : The psychological guidance and the special upbringing : renewals and applications
1- The in danger children and their care.
2- The cooperation between the psychological guide and the psychological doctor.
3- The preparation of the psychological guide and the reformation of its performance.
4- Private classes and their care.
Forth : The measurement and reformation : renewals and applications
1- Tools of measurement and reformation.
2- Reformation of the educational programs and their results.
3- The criteria of the educational approval.
4- The Authentic Evaluation.
Fifth : The management of the education and it's origins
1- Management and technology.
2- Management of the complete quality.
3- Economics of the education.
4- Educational thinking and globalization.
5- Directions and contemporary models in the educational management.

Participation conditions :
1- An abstract of the research project or an abstract of the project it self must be sent including the title and name of the research, number of words must be in the range (200-250) word, the last date for the delivery of the abstracts will be on the first of June 2005, and the researcher will be told by the acceptance of his study in principle within a month from the receipt of the abstract.
2- Three copies of the complete printed research must be sent, and the last date of delivery will be on the fifth of September 2005, and a copy of the research on a computer CD must be attached with, after that the researcher will be told of the decision of the judgment regarding the acceptance of its study for the participation in the conference.
3- The participant that is outside Jordan must pay (200$) to Al-Qahirah Amman – Al Yarmook University – account no. (01/10060064501), regarding that participation may be without submitting a research, and its fees cover the residence, meals and the internal transportations.
4- The participant from inside Jordan must pay (20JD) to Al-Qahirah Amman – Al Yarmook University – account no. (01/10060064501).
5- The participant researches will be published after making modifications on them according to the conference sessions discussions.

The research specifications :
The research will be accepted only if it agrees with the following conditions:
1- Its topic handles one of the conference axes with respect to the experiment, and its future application.
2- It must be original and achieving the conditions of the scientific methodology (APA), and is not published or offered to a magazine or another conference.

Security advices for all university and college professors and lecturers :

1. Protection against kidnapping and killing crimes :

A- look outside the house before getting out, there may be a bomb planted at the door or at the front pavement, and if you have any doubt about any undefined object then call the police immediately using one of the numbers (150 or 160) to send you a bomb expert.
B- Pay attention to all the strangers and cars around your house before leaving for the college, and do the same when you return home, if you have any doubt then call the police.
C- There could be somebody standing to the right or left of the house, holding a mobile to call the kidnapping group about your location and moves, so be careful.
D- When driving a car pay attention to the following :
1) If you have a gun then it must be ready for shooting.
2) In case of having bodyguards, you must pull your trigger to have a bullet ready for shooting.
3) Look to the left and right of the car, watch your back using the car's mirror, your eyes must be concentrated at the car that follow you, usually cars that are involved in these operations have no numbers, or being drove by veiled people, if you feel that you are being watched by them, in this case look at your way with out letting them notice any wrong moves from you.
4) If you observed a car that moves beside you even when you change your direction then try to slow down to know where the car is going to, if you were the goal then immediately defend your self by shooting directly at that car, then try to find a good chance to escape, and try telling the nearest police station that you are being followed by a group of terrorists to let them open fire at the attacking car.
5) Use only roads that many policemen exist in, and do not use the empty or open ways, also do not use roads having a high traffic jam.
6) Try driving on the left side of the road, with the highest speed possible, to have less chance of being chased, as it will be harder to pass by you from the left.

2. Protecting transportation lines :

A- Those who have gun license they must carry their guns with them.
B- Cars with governmental numbers must be switched with "Test Numbers" in order not to be observed by the terrorists.
C- Cars are prohibited from stopping in the public places or crowd streets with out having a good reason to do so.
D- Cars are prohibited from entering alleys as they will be easy targets for the terrorists.
E- Arriving time must be organized for each employer so that the bus will spend less time waiting for the employer.
F- If the bus did not arrive, then employers must separate into small groups, then each group should use public transportation buses to arrive to college.
