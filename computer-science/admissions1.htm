Graduated students from computer science department at Al-Nahrain university had the opportunity to apply for the MSC directly after graduation without getting into the general conditions of application, all students having an average over or equal to 65% were accepted, but unfortunatly this system is no more applicable for the new graduated students after the year (2006-2007), they will apply for all of the general conditions just like other graduates from other universities in iraq, and according to the plan of the university.

The department used to accept a large number of graduates during the past few years and the number had reach to more than 20 applicant each year, that means a ratio of 97%.
