Projects were assigned for the third stage students as a complementary part for their graduation, they were chosen by the department, and their selection depends on the student's grade.

These projects will help students learn how to search for the information, analyse it, and conclude things about the work they did, and this will also help them to be more confident, and dependent on them selves.
